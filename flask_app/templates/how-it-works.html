How It Works

From a single stock symbol to a comprehensive analysis, follow the journey of data through our platform to see how we generate your insights.

A Four-Step Process

We've streamlined complex analysis into a simple, transparent workflow.

1

Enter a Stock Symbol

Your journey begins on our home page. Simply enter the ticker symbol of any US-listed stock you're interested in and submit your query.

2

Gathering Intelligence

Our backend instantly gets to work, fetching real-time data from multiple sources including Google News for professional articles and Reddit for investor discussions.

3

Dual-Channel Analysis

The gathered data is processed in two parallel streams: our trained machine learning models analyze quantitative data for a price prediction, while our sentiment analysis engine scores the qualitative text data.

4

Visualizing Insights

The final analysis is presented on your results page through interactive charts, clear prediction cards, and a live news feed, giving you a comprehensive overview of the stock.
